# Saved Places: Tokyo

- Shiro-Hige's Cream Puff Factory: cute bakery, good for a relaxed afternoon.
- Tsukiji Outer Market: food stalls and breakfast/lunch options.
- Kichijoji: neighborhood with cafes, shops, and Inokashira Park.
- Akihabara: anime and electronics area, but probably overwhelming if scheduled too long.
- Daikanyama T-Site: bookstore and cafe area.
- Yanaka Ginza: older neighborhood, low-key walking area.
