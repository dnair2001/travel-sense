# Past Trip Notes: London

Things I loved:
- Borough Market
- walking along the South Bank
- small bookstores
- afternoon tea
- museums with flexible entry
- neighborhoods like Notting Hill and Shoreditch

Things I did not enjoy:
- trying to visit too many landmarks in one day
- long lines without reservations
- very crowded shopping streets

Travel lesson:
Plan neighborhoods by day instead of crossing the city repeatedly.
