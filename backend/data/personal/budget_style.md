# Budget Style

I prefer moderate-budget trips.

Typical daily budget:
- Breakfast: $8-15
- Lunch: $12-25
- Dinner: $20-45
- Coffee/snacks: $10-20
- Attractions: $20-50 per day

I am willing to splurge on:
- one special dinner
- unique local experiences
- day trips

I avoid:
- luxury hotels
- expensive nightlife
- high-end shopping
