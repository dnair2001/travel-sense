# Travel Preferences

I prefer balanced itineraries with 2-3 major activities per day.

I like:
- street food
- coffee shops
- scenic walks
- bookstores
- local neighborhoods
- museums, but only 1 per day
- parks and gardens
- markets

I dislike:
- packed schedules
- clubbing
- tourist traps
- very expensive tasting menus
- waking up before 8 AM on vacation

I usually prefer public transportation over taxis.
I like having one flexible block each day.
