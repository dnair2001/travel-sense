# New York City Neighborhoods

## Lower Manhattan

Lower Manhattan works well for first-time visitors because it combines waterfront views, historic landmarks, and direct subway access. It is also useful for travelers who want a compact sightseeing day.

## Midtown

Midtown is convenient but can feel crowded and energy-intensive. It suits fast-paced trips that prioritize iconic stops, transit access, and broad restaurant choice over neighborhood calm.

## Upper West Side

The Upper West Side is better for travelers who want museums, park access, and a calmer residential rhythm. It pairs well with Central Park and nearby cultural stops.
